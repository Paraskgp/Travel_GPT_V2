# Seasonal & Time-Bound Experiences

This theme covers experiences that are **only available, meaningfully better, or culturally significant during a specific window of time** — and would be notably different or unavailable at other times of year.

## What belongs here

### Recurring annual events (highest priority)
Sporting events, tournaments, and league matches that the destination is known for hosting:
- Sumo basho (Tokyo — January, May, September)
- Baseball, football, cricket, rugby season matches at iconic stadiums
- Grand Prix, major golf tournaments, cycling races
- Tennis slams and other major sports championships

Music and arts:
- Major music festivals (Coachella, Fuji Rock, Primavera, etc.)
- Seasonal concert series, outdoor cinema seasons
- Annual arts festivals, film festivals, design weeks

Cultural and seasonal:
- Cherry blossom season, autumn foliage peaks (include specific park or viewpoint)
- Festivals and matsuri tied to specific dates (Gion Matsuri, Carnival, Oktoberfest)
- Night markets, summer beach clubs, winter illuminations that only operate seasonally
- Harvest seasons — wine harvest, truffle season, specific food festivals

### Ephemeral natural phenomena
- Seasonal wildlife (whale watching windows, turtle nesting, bioluminescent bays)
- Weather-dependent spectacles (Northern Lights season, monsoon waterfalls)
- Tidal or astronomical events if they define a window (perigee tides, meteor showers)

## What does NOT belong here

- Experiences that are available year-round (they belong in another theme)
- Weather-dependent experiences where the window is "summer" or "not raining" — these go in the relevant activity theme with a `weather_sensitivity` note
- Vague seasonal advice like "autumn is beautiful here" — must tie to a specific named experience

## How to write these cards

The `best_time` field is critical for seasonal cards. It must be:
- Specific dates or a narrow window: "September 7–21" not "September"
- How to get tickets/access if required
- The difference between catching it and missing it

`local_tip` should tell the traveler the thing they'd get wrong: where to sit, how far in advance to book, what session or day of the event is best.

## Prompt for this theme

Think about this travel month specifically. What is actually happening at this destination during this period? Go through:

1. **Is there a major sporting event?** A tournament, a season game, a race? Think broadly — sumo, baseball, football, cricket, basketball, tennis, golf, motorsport.
2. **Is there a music or arts festival?** A known annual event that draws people specifically?
3. **Is there a cultural festival or matsuri?** A specific named event tied to these dates?
4. **Is there a seasonal natural phenomenon?** A specific window for wildlife, bloom, or color?
5. **Is there a seasonal food or drink experience?** A harvest, a market that only runs in this period?

Generate 5–8 cards. Each must be tied to a specific named event or phenomenon — not a vague seasonal category. A card without a specific named anchor does not belong in this theme.
