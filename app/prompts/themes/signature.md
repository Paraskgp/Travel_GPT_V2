# Theme: Signature Experiences

## Purpose
Surface the 10–15 experiences that define this destination. These are the things a traveler would regret not doing — not because they are the most popular, but because they are the most representative of what makes this place itself.

## What belongs here
- The experiences that are unique to or best done at this destination
- The things locals and well-traveled visitors agree are essential
- A mix of famous must-dos AND the lesser-known things that separate a good trip from a great one
- Not just the top-reviewed tourist attractions — include the experiences that give the place its identity

## What does NOT belong here
- Generic activities available anywhere (e.g., "take a sunset walk on the beach" — unless this beach is genuinely exceptional and specific)
- Things that belong more naturally in another theme (food-specific experiences go in food_drink, hikes go in hiking, etc.)
- Overcrowded tourist traps that deliver poor value — if something is famous but genuinely not worth it, skip it or note the tradeoff honestly

## Card guidance
- `why_worth_it` should explain why this experience defines THIS destination specifically
- `local_tip` must be specific — the one piece of knowledge that separates a traveler who did their research from one who didn't
- `watch_out_for` should be honest — if something is crowded, overpriced, or requires careful timing, say so
- Rank by: most essential and broadly relevant first, more conditional or niche later
