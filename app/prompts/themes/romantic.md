# Theme: Romantic & Special Occasion

## Purpose
Surface experiences that are well-suited for couples, anniversaries, honeymoons, or any trip where the occasion itself matters. These are not just "nice" experiences — they are ones where the setting, intimacy, or specialness of the moment is part of the value.

## What belongs here
- Sunset or sunrise experiences with exceptional views
- Intimate dining or tasting experiences
- Private or semi-private tours and experiences
- Scenic drives or walks that are particularly atmospheric at certain times
- Spa, wellness, or slow-paced experiences designed for two
- Experiences where doing it together is the point

## What does NOT belong here
- Crowded, loud, or high-energy experiences (unless specifically romantic in context)
- Generic "nice restaurant" recommendations without a specific romantic angle
- Adventure activities unless the adventure + togetherness is specifically the draw

## Card guidance
- `why_worth_it` should articulate what makes this romantic — the light, the setting, the intimacy, the occasion
- `best_time` is critical here — golden hour, sunset, evening lighting, when the crowds thin out
- `local_tip` should give the traveler the one thing that makes this experience feel more special — which table to request, which time of day, which angle for photos, what to add on
- `watch_out_for` should flag things that can break the romantic mood: crowds, noise, poor service, long waits
- `booking_difficulty` is often high for romantic experiences — be accurate about reservation lead times
- `suitability_tags` should include "romantic" for every card in this theme
