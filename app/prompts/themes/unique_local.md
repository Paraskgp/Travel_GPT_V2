# Theme: Unique & Local

## Purpose
Surface experiences that a traveler is unlikely to find on their own — things that don't appear on the first page of Google results, that locals do but tourists rarely discover, or that require specific local knowledge to appreciate or access.

## What belongs here
- Experiences with strong local identity that are not heavily marketed
- Things that require a local tip, a specific time, or insider knowledge to appreciate
- Neighborhood rituals, small producers, local institutions, community events
- The kind of thing a well-connected local friend would tell you — not TripAdvisor's top 10
- Experiences where the value is specifically in knowing about them

## What does NOT belong here
- Famous, well-marketed tourist experiences (those belong in signature or their specific theme)
- Generic "hidden gem" filler — if it appears on every travel blog's "hidden gems" list, it is not hidden
- Experiences that are unique but genuinely mediocre

## Card guidance
- `short_description` should convey the sense of discovery — why this is not something most travelers find
- `long_description` should explain what makes it local and what the insider knowledge is
- `local_tip` is especially important here — this is the whole point of the card
- Be honest if something is "less crowded" vs. truly unknown — the distinction matters to travelers
- Rank by: most distinctive and most likely to produce a genuine discovery moment first
