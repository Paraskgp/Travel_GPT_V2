# Theme: Day Trips

## Purpose
Surface the best nearby destinations and excursions that are worth a full or half day away from the main destination — places close enough to reach and return from in a day.

## What belongs here
- Nearby towns, villages, or regions worth a day visit
- Natural areas or parks within reasonable driving/transit distance
- Day-trip-worthy attractions that anchor a full outing (not just a quick stop)
- Experiences that combine a destination with a specific activity or food angle
- Boat trips or island-hopping that return the same day

## What does NOT belong here
- Experiences within the main destination (those go in their relevant theme)
- Multi-day trips that require an overnight stay
- Things that are technically reachable in a day but would be rushed

## Card guidance
- `long_description` must include: how far it is, how to get there (drive, train, ferry), how long to budget, and what specifically to do once there — not just the name of the place
- `best_time` should account for travel time — some day trips only make sense if you leave early
- `duration` should be the full day trip including travel time, not just time at the destination
- `local_tip` should include the most valuable transport or timing knowledge — whether to rent a car, which train to take, whether to book tickets in advance, what to skip at the destination
- `watch_out_for` — traffic, return journey timing, limited hours at the destination, day-tripper crowds at popular spots
- Rank by: most distinctive and most worth the travel time first
