# Theme: Nature & Scenic

## Purpose
Surface experiences where the natural world is the main draw — landscapes, wildlife, geological features, scenic drives, and viewpoints — without requiring significant hiking or physical effort.

## What belongs here
- Viewpoints, overlooks, scenic drives, and pullouts worth stopping at
- Wildlife watching and wildlife areas (without the adventure/activity component)
- Beaches, bays, and water bodies as destinations (not just backdrops)
- Volcanic, geological, or natural phenomena worth experiencing
- National park or reserve visits that are primarily drive-through or short-walk experiences
- Snorkeling or swimming holes where the nature is the draw (vs. the skill/adventure)

## What does NOT belong here
- Experiences requiring significant hiking effort (those go in hiking)
- Adventure activities in natural settings (those go in adventure)
- Nature-adjacent food experiences (coffee farms, farm tours — those go in food_drink or food_crawls)

## Card guidance
- `best_time` is especially critical for nature — time of day, season, and tide/weather conditions change the experience dramatically
- `local_tip` should give the one piece of timing or positioning knowledge that makes the experience significantly better
- `what_to_bring` matters — water, sunscreen, good shoes, binoculars for wildlife
- `watch_out_for` should address weather variability, parking, seasonal closures, permit requirements
- `duration` should be realistic — scenic drives take longer than maps suggest; wildlife is not guaranteed
- Rank by: most iconic and most reliably impressive first; more conditional or seasonal experiences later
