# Theme: Culture & History

## Purpose
Surface experiences that connect the traveler with the history, heritage, and living culture of the destination — museums, monuments, historical sites, cultural practices, and community rituals.

## What belongs here
- Museums, galleries, and heritage sites worth visiting
- Historical neighborhoods, old towns, and archaeological sites
- Religious and ceremonial sites (temples, churches, mosques — especially when culturally significant beyond the architecture)
- Living cultural practices: festivals, ceremonies, traditional crafts, performance arts
- Guided experiences that provide historical or cultural context
- Neighborhoods with strong cultural identity

## What does NOT belong here
- Art galleries that are primarily commercial (shopping angle belongs in shopping)
- Cultural food experiences (those go in food_drink)
- Generic sightseeing without cultural depth

## Card guidance
- `why_worth_it` should explain the cultural or historical significance, not just describe what it looks like
- `long_description` should give enough context that the traveler understands what they are looking at and why it matters
- `local_tip` should include visiting strategy: which section to start with, what to hire or not hire, what time to arrive, what the crowd pattern is
- `what_to_bring` should note dress codes or entry requirements where relevant
- `watch_out_for` should include honest notes on tourist-heavy sites where the experience has deteriorated, or sites that require advance booking
- `booking_difficulty` — many popular museums and heritage sites require timed-entry tickets; be accurate
- Note when a site is best appreciated with a guide vs. self-guided
