Target file: app/scripts/eval-board.ts Feedback type: Board eval architecture

Elephant In The Room

The board eval can say a board is good because required names are present and tips sound specific, while still missing the facts needed for safe scheduling.

Major Feedback

Suggested Implementation Direction

  • Add a schedule_ready_count and source_covered_count.
  • Add checks for access_note or future access_constraints on known restricted destinations.
  • Compare board experiences against the candidate inventory by ID once that layer exists.

Principal Engineer Implementation Notes

Board eval should answer: can these cards be trusted and scheduled, not only: do they read well?

Implementation-Ready Checklist

Acceptance Criteria

  • A pretty but unsourced card does not score as schedule-ready.
  • The eval catches missing access/logistics facts before itinerary planning.
  • The output separates copy-quality issues from data-contract issues.

Needs Product Decision

  • Decide the minimum source coverage threshold for a board. Recommendation: 100 percent for scheduled activities, lower is acceptable for inspiration-only cards if visibly marked.
