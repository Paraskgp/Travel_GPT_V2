app/scripts/eval-itinerary.ts Feedback

Target file: app/scripts/eval-itinerary.ts Feedback type: Itinerary eval architecture

Elephant In The Room

The itinerary eval is moving in the right direction with deterministic checks, but the checks are still mostly output-shape checks. It should become a validator for hard travel constraints.

Major Feedback

Suggested Implementation Direction

  • Add a constraint facts input alongside the itinerary and golden file.
  • Score "hard violations" separately from subjective quality.
  • Include a machine-readable failure list that can be consumed by CI or a repair agent.

Principal Engineer Implementation Notes

Itinerary eval should enforce hard constraints deterministically and leave subjective flow to the LLM judge.

Implementation-Ready Checklist

Acceptance Criteria

  • A hard logistics violation fails deterministically without waiting for an LLM judge.
  • The eval can identify whether the problem came from missing facts or bad planner behavior.
  • Flexibility quality is judged separately from feasibility.

Needs Product Decision

  • Decide which violations are hard fail vs warning. Recommendation: safety/logistics impossibility, user-forbidden items, and departure cutoff are hard fail; thin planning notes are warning/quality penalty.
